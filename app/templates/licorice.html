Possesses anti-microbial properties: Liquorice root has anti-microbial properties. The root is particularly good in controlling bacterial strains. Anti-oxidants are some of the very important organic compounds that our body needs. Every day, our body produces free radicals, a certain type of organic chemical residues. These residues can damage our cells and tissues. The electrons release initially can actually perpetuate the further release of electrons from other cells. This can make free radicals a problematic issue. This overtime free radical damage can be reduced by the intake of anti-oxidant foods. The anti-oxidants present in liquorice root help in reducing the free radical activity to a great extent. This is indeed a good news for both pharmaceutical companies as well as for individual concerned about malaria.
