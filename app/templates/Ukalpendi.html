Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Wheat flour (coarse)
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 4 to 5 curry leaves
  • For tempering:- pinch of mustard seeds, 1/4 tsp hing, 1/8 tsp turmeric, 2 dried red chilies
  • 1/2 cup plain sour yogurt, well beaten
  • 1/4 cup water (tip 1)
  • Salt to taste

Method

  • Heat 2 tbsp oil into a pan. Roast wheat flour over medium heat. Roast till color changes to light pink (around 5 minutes). Transfer roasted flour to a bowl. 2) In that same pan, add 1 tbsp oil. Add mustard seeds, hing, turmeric powder, red chilies and curry leaves. Saute for few seconds and add onion. Mix well, add salt and cook onion till translucent. 3) Add roasted flour. Mix nicely for couple of minutes. Mix yogurt and water. Stir well and add to the pan. Stir vigorously as the mixture will become doughy. So, act quickly to avoid lumps. Cover the pan and steam cook over low heat for few minutes. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.
